dataset overview

videogames_wide.csv

This dataset contains video game sales data that span across various paltforms, genres, regions and publishers. Each row contains data of an individual game's release, and measures the millions of units it sold across North America, Europe, Japan, and other regions. Since this data spans across multiple decades, it allows for an analysis looking deeper into industry trends.

Name [categorical]

video game title

While "Name" is a variable, it is unique and used more as an idenitifer to compare other variables against rather than on its own. This is explored more in tandem with Sales.

Platform [categorical]

gaming platform where games released

To my surprise, Nintendo's DS is the platform that appears most frequently in the dataset. Comparatively, its mean and median sales place quite low compared to other platforms. This indicates that although the DS is a popular platform, this does not guarantee/ translate to success in the selling of the games created for the platform.

Genre [categorical]

type of video game

Not as surprising, Action and Sports are the most frequent game genre. This aligns with what I know about the gaming industry from an outsider persepctive, with these genres being more common. Interestingly, when looking at the mean and median genre global sales, Action and Sports are less consistent in sales. Platform being the highest selling genre for both mean and median global sales was quite a surprise!
